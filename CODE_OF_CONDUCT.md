# Code of Conduct

This project expects professional, respectful collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be clear, constructive, and kind.
- Assume good intent while staying precise about technical problems.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Respect privacy. Do not post secrets, private URLs, tokens, or personal information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing another person's private information without permission.
- Posting secrets or vulnerability details in public issues.
- Repeated off-topic or disruptive comments.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; or restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and useful.
